I am a postdoc at INRIA Paris, working with Francis Bach and Gaël Varoquaux.

E-Mail: firstname.lastname@inria.fr (replace ü by u)

Research interests

I am currently interested in supervised learning, active learning, and uncertainty quantification with neural networks. I am interested in improving them on tabular data, but have also worked on interatomic potentials together with Viktor Zaverkin and am generally interested in AI4Science. Previously, I have also worked on theory of neural networks (neural tangent kernels, double descent, benign overfitting) and non-log-concave sampling, as well as other topics.
